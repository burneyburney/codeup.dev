List of National Parks

Acadia

  • Oldest American National Park east of the Mississippi River
  • No hotels or motels within the limits of the park
  • The annual snowfall in Acadia National Park is 60 inches.
  • More than 120 miles of hiking trails

Denali

  • Denali National Park is the site of the highest peak on the continent of North America
  • Denali means "The High One" in language of the Athabascan tribe
  • Those looking for more strenuous exercise sholid hit the Mount Healy Overlook trail.
  • You are likely to see grizzly bears, caribou and moose

Sequoia

  • Every year, more than 1.6 million people visit the park
  • There are 275 caves that have been discovered
  • The sequoias (trees) here grow in a perfectly vertical line
  • One of the more popliar Sequoia National Park tours includes a journey called Trail of 100 Giants
